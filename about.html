About Thrifted Living

Where forgotten furniture finds new purpose.

Our Story

Thrifted Living started in 2018 in a cramped Brooklyn garage with one simple mission: save beautiful furniture from ending up in landfills. What began as a weekend hobby—rescuing worn chairs and tired tables from estate sales—quickly became a passion.

We saw potential where others saw junk. A water-stained dresser became a statement piece. A wobbly mid-century chair was reborn with new upholstery and reinforced joints. Each piece we touched had decades of stories woven into its wood grain, and we wanted to help those stories continue.

Today, our small team of artisans, upholsterers, and furniture lovers has restored over 2,000 pieces. We've grown from that garage into a sunny Brooklyn showroom, but our mission remains the same: give every piece the second chance it deserves.

— Emma & James, Founders

Our Refurbishing Process

Every piece goes through a careful, hands-on restoration journey.

1

Discovery

We source pieces from estate sales, auctions, and private sellers—always looking for quality bones and hidden potential.

2

Assessment

Each piece is carefully inspected. We document its condition, identify repairs needed, and plan the restoration approach.

3

Restoration

Our artisans get to work—repairing joints, refinishing surfaces, replacing springs, and reupholstering with quality fabrics.

4

Final Touch

Every piece is cleaned, polished, and inspected before heading to the showroom floor, ready for its next home.
