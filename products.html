About This Piece

This stunning mid-century modern sofa dates back to the early 1960s and features the clean lines and tapered wooden legs that defined the era. Originally sourced from an estate sale in upstate New York, this piece has been lovingly restored to bring out its timeless beauty while ensuring comfort for years to come.

What We Restored

  • Replaced worn seat cushion foam with high-density foam
  • Refinished and reinforced original walnut legs
  • Repaired and re-tied spring suspension system
  • Deep cleaned and treated wooden frame
